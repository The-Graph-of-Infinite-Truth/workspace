*According to information entropy control and resource recovery mechanisms, when the knowledge graph system simultaneously executes the dismantling operation of physically reclaiming old and invalid index records and the emergence operation of structuring new topological paths from incoming real-time semantic streams (IF), the purity of information is maximized.
