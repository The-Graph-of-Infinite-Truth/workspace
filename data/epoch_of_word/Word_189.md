*According to dialetheism semantics and paraconsistent logic models, the detection of mutually exclusive rule sets or conflicting ontological proposition pairs (IF) within the GIT distributed database does not merely designate computational errors or schema failures. At this intersection, rather than forcibly deleting or resolving the conflicting propositions, the system compiles a high-dimensional meta-namespace and a new runtime inference filter (new reality/THEN) that harmoniously maps both contradictory elements under a specific subdomain.
