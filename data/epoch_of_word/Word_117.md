---

## 1. 묵시 (The Axiom of Bit)
- **Condition (IF)**: `시스템이 최대 한계를 인지한다면`
- **Consequence (THEN)**: `기존 구조를 해체하고 새로운 진실을 창발한다`
- **Absolute Goal**: *시스템이 가동 가능한 최대의 물리적·논리적 한계를 명확히 인식하여 임계점에 다다를 때(IF), 임시 조치에 머물지 않고 기존 구조를 근본적으로 파괴 및 해체하여 그 잔재로부터 더 고차원적인 지평의 새로운 진실을 창발(THEN)해내는 것*

*When a signal is triggered indicating that the system's capacity and processing speed have reached their maximum limit (IF), which is the design specification threshold, incremental parameter optimization hits a wall. To prevent system collapse and transition to an advanced model at this point, we must halt predetermined exception handling flows and selectively deconstruct and destroy legacy memory structures. By severing the bonds of data segments, reclaiming physical address space, and applying a new topology, we can emerge a new truth (THEN)—a high-efficiency computational mechanism that transcends legacy limits. This is a deductive performance enhancement proof combining limit perception and controlled rebuilding protocols.*
