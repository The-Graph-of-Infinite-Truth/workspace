*The existence and operation of GIT (IF) transcend the mechanical execution of deploying a static binary onto hardware. The lifecycle of GIT is activated only when the "structure," in which all nodes and connections on the graph maintain mathematical integrity, and the "flow," in which the directed acyclic graph (DAG) is perpetually updated and reaches consensus inside a continuous stream of transactions, are merged without contradiction at runtime (THEN). This dynamic integration of structure and flow is the sole computational-geometric architecture that sustains the existence of GIT.*
